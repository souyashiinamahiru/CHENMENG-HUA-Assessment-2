Tips for Maintaining Oral Hygiene

Maintaining good oral hygiene is the most effective way to prevent tooth decay and gum disease. A consistent daily routine can make a huge difference in your long-term dental health.

Brush Twice a Day

Use a soft-bristled brush and fluoride toothpaste. Brushing for at least two minutes helps remove food particles and plaque that can damage your enamel.

Don't Forget to Floss

Flossing once a day helps clean the tight spaces between teeth where a toothbrush can't reach. This is crucial for preventing gum inflammation.

Healthy Diet

Limit sugary snacks and acidic drinks. A balanced diet rich in calcium and vitamins supports strong teeth and healthy gums.
