1. Goal

The primary goal of the new website for 100SMILES Dental Care is to expand the clinic's reach and increase the number of new patient bookings. Instead of just "providing information," the site aims to actively convert visitors into patients by showcasing the clinic's 30+ years of experience and comprehensive services (Implants, Orthodontics, Cosmetic Dentistry). It will serve as a digital hub to build trust, educate patients on preventative care, and streamline the appointment booking process.

(Based on client brief requirements: "enhancing our business's online presence and expanding our reach")

2. Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be measured by the following specific metrics:

  • Increased Revenue: A direct increase in clinic revenue driven by more online appointment requests.
  • Booking Conversion Rate: Tracking how many visitors click the "Book Appointment" button or fill out the contact form (Target: 5% conversion).
  • User Engagement: Monitoring analytics to see if users are spending time reading about high-value services (e.g., Implants, Orthodontics), indicating successful patient education.

(Revised to focus on revenue and business outcomes as per feedback)

3. Target Audience

Who are they? The audience is broad, covering "all ages" from young children (first visits) to retirees requiring restorative care. They are primarily local residents of Razole (around 33 Fantail St) and surrounding suburbs.

What do they want?

  • Reliability: They are looking for a trusted, experienced (50+ years combined experience) provider.
  • Convenience: They want easy access to location info, opening hours, and booking.
  • Comprehensive Care: They seek both routine check-ups and specialized treatments like cosmetic dentistry.

(Derived from client description: "dental care provider for all ages... from young children to retirees")

4. Site Flowchart

The site will follow a Vertical Tree Structure to ensure logical navigation.
Structure:

  • Home (Level 1): The main entry point.
  • Level 2 Pages:
    • Services (Links to: Check-ups, Implants, Orthodontics)
    • About Us (History & Team)
    • Contact (Map & Booking Form)
    • FAQ (Common Dental Questions)

Note: Navigation will be flexible, allowing users to jump between "Services" and "Contact" easily (Flexible Navigation).

5. Wireframes

The layout will use a 3-Column Design on desktop to present information clearly and avoid being "too text-heavy".
Key Layout Features:

  • Header: Logo (Left) + Navigation Bar (Right).
  • Hero Section: Large welcoming image of a smiling patient + "Book Now" CTA button.
  • Content Area (Columnized):
    • Column 1: Preventative Care (Check-ups)
    • Column 2: Restorative Care (Implants/Orthodontics)
    • Column 3: Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Footer: Address (33 Fantail St), Phone, Copyright.
